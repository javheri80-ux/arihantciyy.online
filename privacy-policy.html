Privacy & Data Protection Policy

At Arihant City Kalyan Bhiwandi, safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal data is our utmost priority. This document explains our practices regarding the collection, utilization, and protection of the information you share while interacting with our digital platform.

Data Collection Practices

We gather personal details that you willingly submit when completing contact forms, signing up for newsletters, or reaching out to our advisory team. This collected data typically includes your full name, contact number, email address, and individual property preferences.

How We Utilize Your Data

The personal data we acquire is primarily utilized for the following purposes:

  • Delivering comprehensive details regarding the Arihant City Kalyan Bhiwandi project.
  • Promptly addressing your questions, feedback, and site visit requests.
  • Distributing important project milestones, exclusive offers, and pricing updates.
  • Enhancing our website's functionality and optimizing the overall customer journey.

Information Security & Protection

We employ robust security protocols to defend your personal data against unauthorized access or disclosure. We strictly prohibit the selling, trading, or unauthorized sharing of your identifiable information with external entities. We may only share data with verified third-party partners who help us manage our website and business operations, provided they legally bind themselves to strict confidentiality agreements.

Usage of Cookies

To provide a more personalized browsing experience and to monitor website traffic patterns, our site utilizes cookies. You retain the right to disable cookie tracking via your internet browser preferences; however, doing so might limit access to certain interactive functionalities on our platform.

User Agreement & Consent

By accessing and navigating this website, you formally agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Any future modifications or updates to our privacy practices will be transparently published on this webpage.
