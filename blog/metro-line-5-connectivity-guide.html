Metro Line 5: A Game-Changer for Arihant City Kalyan Bhiwandi Residents

Connectivity is the lifeblood of any residential project. For **Arihant City Kalyan Bhiwandi**, the upcoming Metro Line 5 is more than just a transport project—it is a transformation of the entire lifestyle for those living in our 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK flats.

What is Metro Line 5?

Metro Line 5, also known as the Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan corridor, is a 24.9 km long elevated line that will feature 17 stations. This line is designed to bridge the gap between the booming residential zones of Kalyan and the commercial hubs of Thane. One of the key stations on this route is strategically located just 0.2 km from Arihant City, providing residents with "walk-to-metro" convenience.

Seamless Access to Thane and Beyond

Currently, commuting from Bhiwandi to Thane involves navigating heavy traffic on the highway. With the completion of Metro Line 5, this journey will be reduced to mere minutes. Residents will be able to reach Kapurbawdi in Thane in less than 20 minutes, from where they can connect to the Metro Line 4 (Wadala-Kasarvadavali), opening up access to the heart of Mumbai.
