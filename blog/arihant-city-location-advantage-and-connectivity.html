Arihant City Bhiwandi: Location Advantage & Seamless Connectivity

Location is everything in real estate, and Arihant City boasts one of the most strategic locations in the MMR region. Nestled prominently on the Kalyan-Bhiwandi Road, Arihant City offers unparalleled connectivity to major transport and commercial networks.

Connectivity Highlights of Arihant City

Residents of Arihant City enjoy swift travel times and effortless commutes, thanks to a well-developed network of roads and upcoming infrastructure.

  • Upcoming Metro Line 5: The Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan metro line will drastically cut down commute times, making Arihant City a highly connected hub.
  • Highway Access: Arihant City provides rapid access to the Mumbai-Nashik Expressway, ensuring seamless travel to Mumbai and Thane.
  • Proximity to Kalyan Station: One of the busiest railway junctions is just a short drive from Arihant City, linking residents to the central railway network.

Everything Near Arihant City

Beyond travel connectivity, Arihant City places you close to essential social infrastructure. Top schools, multi-specialty hospitals, and entertainment zones are all located within a short radius of Arihant City, providing ultimate convenience for families.
